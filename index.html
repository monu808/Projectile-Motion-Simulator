About Our Project

Welcome to the "Projectile Motion Simulator" project! This web-based application is a practical demonstration of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in action, enhanced by the integration of the Chart.js library. Designed to illustrate the fundamental principles of projectile motion, this simulator allows users to interactively explore and visualize the motion of launched objects. With just a few inputs, you can simulate and observe the trajectory, height, range, and time of flight of a projectile, making the concept of projectile motion more accessible and engaging. Dive in and experience the world of physics through this dynamic and user-friendly tool!
